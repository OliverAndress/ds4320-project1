## Problem Definition

**General Problem:**

 Forecasting Global Climate Change - General Problem #4 from DS 4320 Project 1 rubric

**Refined Specific Problem:**

Predicting the success of voluntary carbon offset projects. By using the reforestation initiatives as either Effective or Underperforming based on their registry affiliation, geographic region, and project type. 

**Rationale Paragraph**

Doing something as broad and as complex as Forecasting Global Climate Change is a massive and multifaceted domain. It would also require multi-decade time-series data which is very difficult to structure into a traditional relational model for the scope of this project. By narrowing the scope into looking at the Voluntary Carbon Market, the wide issue can become a classification problem. 


**Motivation Paragraph**

I took a half-semester class this semester on Climate Investing. I learned a lot but the two things that particularly stood out was the impending danger of climate change on all walks of life as well as the ability for young people to have the ability to make the world a better place while also making money. We also talked a lot about the benefits for Carbon Credits, where corporations or organizations buy carbon credits to offset their emissions. However, many projects fail to protect forests leading to a term called Greenwashing (where companies outwardly appear to be environmentally friendly when they are not). I want to use what I have learned in my class and through my Data Science Major to try and predict a projects success before capital is committed. This ensures that money is efficently allocated to the initiatives that will help make the world cleaner, therefore helping reduce the impact of climate change.
